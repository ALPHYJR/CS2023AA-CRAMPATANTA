My Favorite character In Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuki Tsukumo is a character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series. She is one of only four special grade jujutsu sorcerers. Yuki demonstrates exceptional combat instincts and exhibits remarkable proficiency in jujutsu, effectively employing her innate technique, barrier techniques, and domain expansion. Moreover, she excels in mentoring students and nurturing outstanding potential, despite not holding a formal teaching position at Jujutsu High.
