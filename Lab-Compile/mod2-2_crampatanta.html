Jujutsu Kaisen

"Jujutsu Kaisen" is a popular Japanese anime and manga series. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who unwittingly becomes a host to a powerful curse. He joins the Jujutsu Sorcerers to combat curses and collect dangerous artifacts, all while uncovering the mysteries of a dark, supernatural world. Action-packed and filled with unique curses and magic battles, it explores themes of fear, death, and the human condition.
